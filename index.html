📖 Farm Manual

Everything you need to know to become a master farmer!

💧 Watering Guide

  • Level 0 (🔴 Dry) — growth slows down, risk of drought death
  • Level 1–2 (💧 OK) — healthy growth, best quality chance
  • Level 3 (🟣 Excess) — rot risk each day

💡 Water once per day for ideal balance 💡 Skip watering on Rainy days — it's free!

🌿 Fertilizer Guide

  • Adds +1 day of growth progress instantly
  • Using once → quality bonus possible
  • Using twice → over-fertilized, quality may drop
  • Costs 5g per use

💡 Fertilize once per crop for best results

🌦️ Weather Events

  • ☀️ Sunny — normal growth
  • 🌧️ Rain — auto-waters all crops free!
  • ⛈️ Storm — 30% chance each crop gets damaged
  • 🏜️ Drought — water level drops twice per day

💡 Check today's weather before manually watering

⭐ Crop Quality System

Quality affects the final sell price:

  • ⬇️ Low = 50% of market price
  • ➡️ Normal = 100% of market price
  • ⬆️ High = 150% of market price

Quality improves with good watering, one fertilizer dose, and planting in the crop's best season.

🔄 Multi-Harvest Crops

Blueberry and Strawberry regrow after each harvest — no replanting needed! Great value per seed cost.


⏰ Crop Rot Timer

Ready crops rot in 3 days if not harvested. Rotten crops cannot be sold — clear them with the 🗑️ tool.

💡 Check your farm every day!

📈 Market & Events

  • Prices fluctuate ±20% every day based on supply
  • Random events double or triple a specific crop's price for 2–3 days
  • High quality crops earn 50% more
💡 Stockpile crops, sell during price spike events!

🏗️ Farm Expansion

Start with 10 plots. Unlock 5 more at a time by clicking "Expand Farm". Each expansion costs more than the last.

More plots = more crops = more profit!

💡 Expand early to scale up faster
